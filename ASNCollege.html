Our History

Located in Tenali, Andhra Pradesh, Annabattuni Satyanarayana Degree College was established in 1988. Annabattuni Satyanarayana Degree College, a leading institute in higher education, offers Diploma and Degree courses to students. These courses are provided at the UG and PG levels across IT & Software, Engineering, Science and Accounting & Commerce streams. The institute offers courses in Full Time mode. The institute is famous for offering courses such as B.Com, B.Sc., BCA, M.Sc., PG Diploma and UG Diploma. Annabattuni Satyanarayana Degree College students are taught by faculty who are trained professionals in fields such as Computer Science Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science.To avail of this quality education, students must pay fee ranging between INR 36,000-54,000. With this institute, students can enjoy studying in the state of the art infrastructure.
